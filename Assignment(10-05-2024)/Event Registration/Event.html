About The Event

TechFest is an annual gathering of visionaries, technologists, and industry leaders coming together to explore the latest trends, innovations, and disruptions shaping our digital future. Join us for two days of insightful talks, interactive workshops, and networking opportunities.

Event Details

Date: 11th and 12th May, 2024

Location: N Convention, Madhapur, Hyderabad.

Theme: "Unlocking the Potential of Emerging Technologies"

Chief Guests

N.R.Narayana Murthy

Founder of Infosys

Nagavara Ramarao Narayana Murthy is an Indian billionaire businessman. He is one of the seven co-founders of Infosys, and has previously served as the chairman, chief executive officer, president, and chief mentor of the company before retiring and taking the title chairman emeritus.

Ratan Naval Tata

Former chairman of Tata Group

Ratan Naval Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist and former chairman of Tata Sons. He was a chairman of the Tata Group from 1990 to 2012, and interim chairman from October 2016 through February 2017. He continues to head its charitable trusts.
